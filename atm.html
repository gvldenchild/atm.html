Using ATM Machines

There are several transactions you can do an ATM:

• Deposits

• Withdrawals

• Balance Inquiry

• And new, to my bank at least, purchase stamps

The steps required for withdrawing money using an ATM Machine

1. Insert card in Machine.

2. When prompted, type you secret PIN Number.

3. Press ENTER. The machine verifies your PIN and prompts you to choose the amount of money to withdraw.

4. Type of select the amount of money to withdraw.

.5. Press ENTER. IF correct, or click CANCEL and retype or select the correct amount.

When you're done, the system prints a receipt, returns your card, and gives you the money requested.
